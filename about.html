my journey

I began my journey at St. John's University with a MS in Education specializing in School Counseling. Shortly after I continued my journey by becoming a certified life coach through the Fowler International Academy of Professional Coaching.

I have experience in helping individuals at the elementary, high school, collegiate and career transitioning levels. I've grown my practice on building positive, trusting and respectful relationships with my clients.

My holistic approach is effective for assisting clients in becoming their best version of themselves by engaging and developing the whole self.

My motto is to live life to the fullest. I pride myself everyday to live by this motto and to make sure my clients live by this as well. Therefore, I have a strong passion for assisting others in achieving their personal goals.
